## 🎨 UNIVERSAL DESIGN ELEMENTS

### Apply to ALL 7 Images:

**Logo Placement:**
- Top-left OR top-right corner
- Size: 140-160px width
- Must be recognizable at small size

**Text Hierarchy:**
- **Main Headline:** 60-72px bold (white)
- **Subheading:** 28-36px medium (orange accent)
- **Body/Tagline:** 16-24px regular (white or light gray)

**Colors Used:**
- Primary Orange (#FF6B35)
- Secondary Indigo (#2E4B7F)
- Accent Sakura (#E85D75)
- Dark Background (#1A1A1A)
- White Text (#FFFFFF)

**Layout Rules:**
- Dimensions: 1200×630px EXACTLY
- Safe margin: 15px from all edges
- Critical content: Keep in center zone
- Maximum text lines: 3-4 lines
- Logo contrast: Ensure visibility

## ✨ PRO TIPS FOR BETTER DESIGNS

1. **Test at Thumbnail Size**
   - Preview at 200×105px
   - Text must be readable
   - Logo must be visible

2. **High Contrast is Key**
   - Ensure text pops from background
   - White on dark = best for readability
   - Avoid similar color text/background

3. **Brand Consistency**
   - Use JIBB colors consistently
   - Include logo on every image
   - Maintain visual hierarchy

4. **Don't Overcrowd**
   - Max 3-4 main elements
   - Give elements breathing room
   - Use white space effectively

5. **Test Before Upload**
   - Check on Facebook Debugger
   - Verify on Twitter Card Validator
   - Ensure file size is under 1MB
   - Verify exact dimensions
